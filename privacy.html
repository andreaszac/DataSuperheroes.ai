Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 30/12/2024

At DataSuperheroes.ai, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard the information we gather from your visit to our website.
Information We Collect:
1. Non-Personal Information:
- We collect non-personal information through Google Analytics, including browser type, operating system, device type, anonymized IP address, geographic location, referring websites, and browsing behavior such as pages viewed and time spent on our site.
2. Cookies:
- Google Analytics uses cookies to track user behavior on our website. These cookies help us improve your experience and monitor website performance.
How We Use Your Information:
1. Analytics and Insights:
- To analyze website traffic, understand visitor behavior, and improve our website’s functionality and content.
2. Marketing and Performance Monitoring:
- To measure the effectiveness of marketing campaigns and identify areas for improvement.
Data Security:
1. Protection Measures:
- We use industry-standard measures to protect the data collected through our website.
2. Data Retention:
- Data collected via Google Analytics is retained for as long as necessary to fulfill its purpose, subject to Google’s retention policies.
Your Choices:
1. Cookie Preferences:
- You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings or decline them via our website’s cookie banner.
2. Opt-Out of Analytics:
- You can opt out of Google Analytics tracking by using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.
Children’s Privacy:
- Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children without parental consent.
Changes to This Privacy Policy:
- We may update this Privacy Policy periodically. Any changes will be reflected on this page with the updated date.
Contact Us:
- If you have questions or concerns regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at info@datasuperheroes.ai.
